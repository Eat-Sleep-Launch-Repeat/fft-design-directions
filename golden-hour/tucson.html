The Buildings That Do the Talking

Three landmarks, three reasons to stay downtown after dark

The Downtown Tucson Haunted History Tour walks past all three. Each one earned its place the hard way.

✦ Est. 1919

Hotel Congress

In January 1934, a basement fire sent every guest down the fire escapes, and one firefighter walked away with a $12 tip and a face worth remembering. That story made the papers. The woman in white of Room 242 never did.

✦ Est. 1930

Fox Tucson Theatre

A movie palace that opened as the Depression closed in, went dark for decades and came back with its marquee blazing. Ask your guide who stayed behind through the dark years.

Hear it on the Ghost Tour →
✦ Stone Avenue

St. Augustine Cathedral

Look close at the facade: saguaro, yucca and a horned toad carved in stone beside the saints. The desert earned a place on the cathedral. What happened in its shadow is a longer story, and your guide saves it for after dark.

Before You Book Tucson

Quick answers

Where do the Tucson tours meet?+
Both Tucson tours meet at Hotel Congress, 311 E Congress St, on the Toole Avenue side by the "Visit Hotel Congress" mural. The hotel is a landmark on the route, so you start the tour standing inside the first story. Your confirmation email pins the exact spot, and you can call or text (928) 224-0518 if you get turned around.
Is 8 PM too late in the Tucson heat?+
The opposite. The 8 PM start is timed for after the desert cools, when downtown is at its best and the neon is doing its job. Wear comfortable shoes, bring water in summer, and expect an easy 90 minute walk through the downtown core.
Which Tucson tour should we pick?+
Most visitors take the 8 PM haunted history tour: 8 haunted stops, including Hotel Congress, the Fox Tucson Theatre and St. Augustine Cathedral. For daylight, the mural tour covers work by artists like Ignacio Garcia, Joe Pagac and Fin DAC, every story researched and verified. Our guides customize every walk. They know which pieces just went up, which artist is working on the next project, and the stories only locals hear. Cancel free up to 72 hours out, so booking both is low risk.
