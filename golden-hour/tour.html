The Story

Some guests of the Hotel Monte Vista never checked out

In 1927 the town of Flagstaff pooled its money and built itself a hotel. Nearly a century later, the Hotel Monte Vista still rents rooms, and a few longtime residents still refuse to leave. The phantom bellboy knocks on doors on the third floor. A rocking chair in Room 305 moves for no one. Our guides will tell you who they were, when it happened, and which window to watch on your way home.
