Before You Book Flagstaff

Quick answers

Where do the Flagstaff tours meet?+
The ghost tour and Mountain Town of Madness meet at Wheeler Park, 212 W Aspen Ave, near City Hall. The Route 66 Centennial Walking Tour meets at Heritage Square. The mural tour meets at the Orpheum Theater Box Office, 15 W Aspen Ave, and the pub crawl meets at the Flagstaff Visitor Center, 1 E Route 66. Your confirmation email pins the exact spot, and our office is at 120 N San Francisco St Unit J in downtown Flagstaff if you get turned around.
Which tour should we pick for a first visit?+
Most first-timers take the 7 PM ghost tour, then come back the next evening for Route 66 at golden hour. Traveling with kids? The mural tour on a Thursday through Saturday morning is an easy win. Mountain Town of Madness is 18 and over, and the pub crawl is strictly 21 and over.
What about Flagstaff weather?+
We run rain, snow or starlight, and at 7,000 feet the evenings cool off fast even in summer. Bring a layer. Full refund when you cancel 72 or more hours before your tour, and we are happy to move your date when plans shift. Call or text (928) 224-0518.
