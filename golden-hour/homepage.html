Before You Book

Quick answers

Is the ghost tour scary, or can I bring my kids?+
Our classic tours are storytelling, not jump scares. All ages are welcome and we recommend 13+ for the ghost tours. Two exceptions: Mountain Town of Madness is 18 and over, and the Spirits With The Spirits pub crawl is 21 and over.
What if it rains or snows?+
We run rain, snow or starlight. Flagstaff weather is part of the show. Dress in layers and we will handle the ghosts.
Can I cancel or reschedule?+
Yes. Full refund when you cancel 72 or more hours before your tour, and we are happy to move your date when plans shift. Call or text (928) 224-0518.
Where do the tours start?+
Each tour page lists its exact meeting point. Flagstaff's original ghost tour meets at Wheeler Park, 212 W Aspen Ave, near City Hall. Our office is at 24 N San Francisco St Unit J in downtown Flagstaff.
