Why Trust a Tour Company's Travel Guides

We grade ghost towns the way we build tours

Since 2015 we have researched, written and led our own tours. That means primary sources, named buildings, real dates, and a working knowledge of which "haunted" stops are just good lighting. These guides apply the same standard to every city below.

How we work: we name the saloon, the year and the character, then tell you where to stand. When a legend does not hold up, we say so. When another city's tour is worth your money, we say that too.
