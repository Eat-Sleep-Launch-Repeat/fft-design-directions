Before the Curtain

FLAGSTAFF QUESTIONS

What if it rains or snows?
Tours run rain, snow or starlight. Flagstaff sits at 7,000 feet and the weather is part of the show. Bring a layer, even in July, and we will handle the rest.
Where do Flagstaff tours meet?
Everything departs from downtown Flagstaff, an easy walk from the hotels and Heritage Square. Your exact meeting spot is on your confirmation, and our office at 24 N San Francisco St Unit J is two blocks away if you get turned around.
Can I cancel?
Full refund when you cancel 72 or more hours before your tour. Plans change, we get it.
