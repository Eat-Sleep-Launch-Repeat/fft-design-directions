TUCSON QUESTIONS

Is the Tucson Ghost Tour kid-friendly?
Yes. We deal in history and goosebumps, not gore. Kids who like a good ghost story do great, and the route is stroller-friendly downtown sidewalk the whole way. Adult tickets are $29; parents know their own crew best.
What time do Tucson tours run?
The ghost tour departs evenings at 8 PM this summer, after the desert heat breaks and the neon takes over. It runs about 90 minutes. The mural tour runs on select dates in daylight, when the walls do the glowing.
Where do Tucson tours meet?
Both tours meet at Hotel Congress, 311 E Congress St, in the heart of downtown. It is easy to find: look for the 1919 hotel with the best story on the block. Your confirmation has the exact spot, and you can call or text (520) 220-5616 if you get turned around.
