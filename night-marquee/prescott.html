Before the Curtain

PRESCOTT QUESTIONS

Where do Prescott tours meet?
Both tours depart from the Rough Rider Statue on Courthouse Plaza, 120 S Cortez St, in the heart of downtown Prescott. Whiskey Row is directly across the street, and your exact departure details are on your confirmation.
What if the weather turns?
Tours run rain, snow or starlight. Prescott sits a mile high and the evenings cool off fast, even in summer, so bring a layer for the 7 PM ghost tour. The weather has been part of this town's story since 1864.
Can I cancel?
Full refund when you cancel 72 or more hours before your tour. Plans change, we get it.
