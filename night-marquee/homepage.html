QUICK QUESTIONS

Is this scary, or is it history?
Both, on purpose. Every stop is documented local history first: the saloon, the year, the names. The ghosts are how the town remembers it. All ages are welcome on most tours, recommended 13+ for the ghost routes.
What if it rains or snows?
Tours run rain, snow or starlight. Flagstaff weather is part of the show. Dress for the mountain and we will handle the rest.
Can I cancel?
Full refund when you cancel 72 or more hours before your tour. Plans change, we get it.
How big are the groups?
Small by design, capped at 28 so everyone can hear the story and see the window it happened behind.
