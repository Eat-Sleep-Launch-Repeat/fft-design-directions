Opinion: A Hundred Cities, One Script. Or One Family, Five Generations.

From the Publisher's Desk

National ghost tour chains sell the same tour in a hundred cities. Same script, same beats, same "haunted" adjectives, written in an office a thousand miles from the buildings they describe. We are the opposite, and we are proud of it.

Freaky Foot Tours is a mother and son operation, founded in Flagstaff in 2015, with five generations of Arizona roots behind it. Co-founder Susan Johnson did not skim the archives; she literally wrote the book on haunted Flagstaff. When our guides point at the Weatherford Hotel, they are pointing at a building they walk past on the way to get coffee.

Our guides live in the towns they haunt. They know which bartender saw the glass slide, which room the front desk quietly stops booking in October, and which stories are too good to be true, so we do not tell those. That is the whole difference. That is why 30,000+ guests and 1,400+ verified reviews later, we are still the local paper of record for the strange and the storied.
