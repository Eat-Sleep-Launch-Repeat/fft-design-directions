Before you meet the dead

Good Questions

Is the ghost tour too scary for kids?

Our tours are all ages and we recommend 13+ for the ghost tours. The Mountain Town of Madness tour is strictly 21 and up. History and mural tours are great for the whole crew.

What happens if it rains or snows?

We run rain, snow or starlight. Flagstaff weather is part of the show, so bring a layer and comfortable shoes.

What is the refund policy?

Full refund when you cancel 72 or more hours before your tour. Need to move your date? Call or text us at (928) 224-0518 and we will sort it out.

Where do the tours meet?

Each tour has its own corner of downtown. The Original Flagstaff Ghost Tour meets at Wheeler Park, 212 W Aspen Ave, near City Hall. Your confirmation includes an exact pin.
