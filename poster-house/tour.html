Stop 01 · Hotel Monte Vista

The Bellboy Who Still Knocks

The Monte Vista opened in 1927 and some of the staff apparently never clocked out. Guests still report a phantom bellboy announcing room service to empty hallways, and that is just the polite floor. Your guide will tell you which rooms the regulars ask to avoid.

Stop 02 · The Orpheum Theater

A Full House After Closing

Flagstaff's grand old movie house has been packing seats for over a century. Staff closing up alone tell of footsteps in the balcony and equipment with a mind of its own. We will let the theater speak for itself from the sidewalk out front.

Stop 03 · A 1930s Cold Case

The Case Susan Wrote The Book About

In 1937 the Walkup family murders stunned Flagstaff, and the questions never fully settled. Our co-founder Susan Johnson literally wrote the book on haunted Flagstaff and shared this story on Travel Channel's The Dead Files in 2022. You will hear it steps from where it happened, along with the Weatherford Hotel and the other guests who never checked out.
